Lifecycle Diagram by Pledge Continuity

Key Dates of Pledge :

  • Created : Date when pledge was created [Become Exist]
  • Start Date : Due Date of First Instalment [Become Active]
  • End Date : Due Date of Last Instalment (not payment date of last instalment) [Become Closed (only apply to Finite Pledges) ]
  • On Hold Date : Most Recent Date when pledge was put on hold [Become On Hold]
  • Written Down Date : Date when pledge was cancelled [Become Cancelled]
  • Next Call Date : Date when a Finite Pledge conceptually finished [Become Finished (only apply to Finite Pledges) ]
  • Instalment Due Dates : Calculated Due Date of each instalment
  • Instalment Payment Dates : Date of Payment (in certain Batch) of each instalment (Privenue Reporting Date)
  • Instalment Batch Approved Dates : Date of Batch Approved Date of each instalment
